About Weza Wallet

Empowering Financial Literacy for All

Weza Wallet is a platform designed to promote financial literacy and empower individuals to make smarter financial decisions. With a focus on both youth and elderly communities, Weza Wallet connects users to tools and resources that make financial education accessible and engaging.

Why We Exist

In Kenya, many people, including the youth and elderly, face challenges when it comes to managing money effectively. Despite the availability of financial resources like M-Akiba, SACCOs, and bank programs, financial literacy remains low. Weza Wallet aims to bridge this gap by providing simple, relatable, and practical financial education.

Our Mission

To empower individuals by providing the knowledge and tools they need to build a secure financial future.

Key Features

  • **Educational Resources:** Simplified content on saving, investing, and budgeting.
  • **Interactive Learning:** Financial quizzes and challenges for engaging education.
  • **Community Support:** Stories, tips, and shared experiences to inspire growth.

Our Approach: Design Thinking

Weza Wallet uses a user-centered approach to solve the problem of financial illiteracy. By empathizing with the challenges faced by both the youth and the elderly, we design tools and content that truly meet their needs. We listen, ideate, prototype, and test continuously to ensure an impactful solution.
